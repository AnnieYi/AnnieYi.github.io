WHAT ARE MY PROFESSIONAL INTERESTS?
At UC Berkeley, I'm currently studying Economics and Statistics. Academically, I'm interested in applying statistics and economics to research the causes of poverty in this world and how to develop effective economic policies that encourage equality in less developed countries. That's the direction I'm looking forward to in terms of research. On the business side, I'm interested in the intersection of creativity and analysis, whether that be through user interface and user experience research or just developing creative models to help improve inefficient or sub-optimal business models. Since I also love art and graphics design, I'm looking to maybe expand my website with a section on food blogging, as that combines two of my less professional passions in life.
WHAT DO YOU CARE ABOUT THE MOST IN LIFE?
There are a lot of things I care about in life, but for me life is an endless journey of discovering unexpected sunny trifles. I care about family, and I choose to reflect on each event that crosses my narrative - perhaps that's why I've always wanted to eventually write a book. I guess the fact that I found it necessary to include this section in my About Me says something about how much I care. I care about making mistakes and looking back on them so I won't make the same ones again. A lot of my artwork reflectswhat is on my mind at a certain moment in time, or perhaps what concerns me most about life's caprice. Now on another note, as you might be able to tell from the section above, I really care about food. Food to me is possibly the most beautiful expression of art, touching upon both taste and sight. Hit me up anytime and I'll always be down to explore new restaurants, meet new faces and talk about life.
